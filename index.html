The Problem
Currently 7,000 students are dropping out of school every day (nationally).
1.2 million students drop out annually. (Source: http://www.whitehouse.gov/the-press-office)

The Solution
CHANGE STUDENT TRAJECTORY! K12tocollege™ youth programs develop character, real life skills and a sense of responsibility and community. These goals are achieved through the implementation of time tested, proven methods (mentoring, employment internships and continued education services), creatively combined with our fun programs that incorporate popular social media services like Facebook, Twitter & Instagram.

Our Strategy


FREE help to succeed in life!

We will utilize popular social media trends to meet our goals. In 2017 our programs will begin to generate New Funding for hundreds of “starter deposits” for new SW Missouri student college savings plans.

Board of Governance


K12toCollege™ is a community enhancement organization that is a member of National Christian Foundation. Our board is currently seeking a few more retired and active educators, community professionals and leaders to sit on our board to improve effectiveness and efficiencies of our programs.

The Economic Engine


Our mission and business model promotes a givers-gain philosophy that produces increased profits through improved foot traffic (and web traffic) to our sponsor businesses. Local and national businesses get even more exposure by making a tax deductible donation to K12toCollege™ or choose to be an advisor. These funds will immediately go to work creating college saving plans, life skills learning through our internships and youth sports programs. Additionally, long-term positive economic impact for SW Missouri communities are anticipated.

K12toCollege™ anticipates test marketing of our community enhancement programs for Taney, Christian and Greene County to begin in 2017.
